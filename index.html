Rules:

Ping Pong will count from 1 up to the provided number but when:

  1. Numbers are divisible by 3, Ping is printed instead
  2. Numbers are divisible by 5, Pong is printed instead
  3. Numbers are divisible by 3 and 5, Ping-Pong is printed instead
